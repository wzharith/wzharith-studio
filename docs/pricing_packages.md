## Booking Terms

### Deposit & Payment
- **Deposit**: 50% upon booking confirmation
- **Balance**: Due 3 days before event
- **Payment Methods**: Bank transfer, DuitNow, Cash

### Cancellation Policy
- 30+ days before event: Full refund minus admin fee (RM 50)
- 14-29 days before event: 50% refund
- Less than 14 days: No refund (deposit retained)

### What's Included
- Professional sound equipment (portable speaker + wireless system)
- Transport within Klang Valley
- Sound check before event
- Coordination with wedding planner/emcee

### Requirements from Client
- Confirmed event timeline
- Performance area/stage
- Power supply (if using our equipment)
- Song requests at least 1 week before event
