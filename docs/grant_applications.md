# WZHarith Music - Grant & Funding Opportunities

## Overview

As a registered SSM enterprise in the creative industry, you're eligible for various grants and funding programs. This document outlines the key opportunities and application requirements.

---

## 1. TEKUN Nasional

### About
TEKUN provides micro-financing for Bumiputera entrepreneurs. Ideal for equipment purchases and working capital.

### Funding Amount
- **Skim Pembiayaan TEKUN**: Up to RM 50,000
- **Skim Pembiayaan Mikro**: RM 1,000 - RM 10,000 (easier approval)

### Eligibility
- [ ] Malaysian citizen (Bumiputera)
- [ ] Age 18-65 years old
- [ ] Registered business (SSM)
- [ ] Not a bankrupt

### What You Can Use It For
- Professional saxophone upgrade
- Sound equipment (speaker, wireless system)
- Recording equipment
- Marketing and branding
- Working capital

### Required Documents
- [ ] SSM Certificate
- [ ] MyKad copy
- [ ] Business plan (simple)
- [ ] Bank statements (3 months)
- [ ] Proof of business activities (photos, invoices)

### How to Apply
1. Visit [www.tekun.gov.my](https://www.tekun.gov.my)
2. Create account or visit nearest TEKUN office
3. Submit application with documents
4. Attend interview (if required)
5. Await approval (2-4 weeks)

### Contact
- Website: www.tekun.gov.my
- Hotline: 03-2610 0100
- Find nearest branch: Use branch locator on website

## 2. PUNB (Perbadanan Usahawan Nasional Berhad)

### About
PUNB provides financing specifically for Bumiputera entrepreneurs with growth potential.

### Funding Amount
- **Dana Niaga**: RM 20,000 - RM 100,000

### Eligibility
- [ ] Malaysian Bumiputera
- [ ] Registered business (minimum 1 year recommended)
- [ ] Good business track record
- [ ] Viable business plan

### What You Can Use It For
- Business expansion
- Equipment upgrade
- Marketing campaigns
- Platform development
- Hiring staff/collaborators

### How to Apply
1. Visit [www.punb.com.my](https://www.punb.com.my)
2. Submit online application
3. Prepare business plan
4. Attend presentation/interview
5. Approval process (4-8 weeks)

### Contact
- Website: www.punb.com.my
- Hotline: 03-2050 2000

## 3. MyCreative Ventures

### About
MyCreative is under the Ministry of Communications & Multimedia, specifically supporting creative industry entrepreneurs.

### Funding Types
- **Equity Investment**: For scalable creative businesses
- **Grants**: Project-based funding
- **Soft Loans**: Low-interest financing

### Eligibility
- [ ] Malaysian-owned company (51%+)
- [ ] Creative industry focus
- [ ] Scalable business model
- [ ] Strong value proposition

### Best For
- Building your digital platform
- Creating educational content/courses
- Developing artist collaboration network
- Innovative creative projects

### How to Apply
1. Visit [www.mycreative.com.my](https://www.mycreative.com.my)
2. Review current programs
3. Submit pitch deck/business proposal
4. Attend pitching session
5. Due diligence and approval

### Contact
- Website: www.mycreative.com.my
- Email: info@mycreative.com.my

## 4. CENDANA (Cultural Economy Development Agency)

### About
CENDANA supports arts and cultural practitioners with grants, programs, and professional development.

### Grant Programs
- **Arts Grants**: Project-based funding for arts initiatives
- **Emergency Fund**: Support during difficult times
- **Professional Development**: Training and upskilling

### Eligibility
- [ ] Malaysian citizen
- [ ] Active arts practitioner
- [ ] Registered with CENDANA database

### What You Can Apply For
- Recording/producing original music
- Workshops and educational programs
- Arts collaboration projects
- Equipment for arts practice

### How to Register & Apply
1. Visit [www.cendana.com.my](https://www.cendana.com.my)
2. Register in the CENDANA database (free)
3. Check for open grant calls
4. Submit application during open period
5. Panel review and approval

### Contact
- Website: www.cendana.com.my
- Email: info@cendana.com.my

## 5. SME Corp Programs

### About
SME Corp offers various programs for registered SMEs including training, matching grants, and advisory services.

### Relevant Programs
- **SME Digitalisation Grant**: Up to RM 5,000 for digital adoption
- **SCORE Program**: Business advisory and matching
- **Training Programs**: Free/subsidized courses

### Eligibility
- [ ] Registered with SSM
- [ ] Meet SME definition (annual sales < RM 50 million)

### How to Access
1. Visit [www.smecorp.gov.my](https://www.smecorp.gov.my)
2. Register as SME
3. Access available programs
4. Apply for suitable grants/training

## Application Timeline & Strategy

### Recommended Approach

| Priority | Grant | Amount | Purpose | Apply When |
|----------|-------|--------|---------|------------|
| 1 | TEKUN Mikro | RM 5-10K | Equipment upgrade | Month 1-2 |
| 2 | SME Digital Grant | RM 5K | Platform/website | Month 2-3 |
| 3 | CENDANA Registration | - | Join database | Month 1 |
| 4 | MyCreative | Varies | Platform scaling | Month 6+ |
| 5 | PUNB Dana Niaga | RM 50K+ | Major expansion | Year 2+ |

### Tips for Success

1. **Strong Business Plan**: Even for small grants, show clear vision
2. **Track Record**: Document all past performances with photos/testimonials
3. **Financial Records**: Keep clean accounting from day 1
4. **Social Proof**: Build social media presence before applying
5. **Be Specific**: Show exactly how you'll use the funds
6. **Follow Up**: Don't just submit - follow up on applications
