## Registration Steps

### Step 1: Create MySSM Account
- [ ] Visit [ezbiz.ssm.com.my](https://ezbiz.ssm.com.my)
- [ ] Click "Daftar Pengguna Baharu" (Register New User)
- [ ] Complete registration with MyKad number
- [ ] Verify email address

### Step 2: Name Search (RM15 per search)
- [ ] Login to ezbiz portal
- [ ] Navigate to "Carian Nama" (Name Search)
- [ ] Search for "WZHarith Music"
- [ ] If available, proceed to registration
- [ ] If taken, search alternative names

### Step 3: Business Registration
- [ ] Select "Pendaftaran Perniagaan Baharu" (New Business Registration)
- [ ] Fill in business details:
  - Business name (as approved from search)
  - Business code: 90001
  - Business address
  - Commencement date
- [ ] Upload required documents
- [ ] Pay registration fee

### Registration Fees
| Duration | Fee |
|----------|-----|
| 1 year | RM30 |
| 2 years | RM60 |
| 5 years | RM150 |

**Recommendation**: Start with 1-2 years, renew after establishing the business.

### Step 4: Receive Certificate
- [ ] Download digital SSM certificate (Perakuan Pendaftaran Perniagaan)
- [ ] Print and display at business premises (if applicable)
- [ ] Save digital copy for banking and official purposes

## Post-Registration Tasks

### Banking (Within 1 Week)
- [ ] Open business savings account with SSM certificate
- [ ] Recommended banks: Maybank, CIMB, RHB, Bank Islam
- [ ] Documents needed:
  - SSM Certificate
  - MyKad
  - Initial deposit (usually RM100-500)

### LHDN Registration (Within 1 Month)
- [ ] Register for income tax (if not already registered)
- [ ] Report business income under Form B (self-employed)
- [ ] Keep records of:
  - All income received
  - Business expenses (equipment, transport, backing tracks)
  - Receipts and invoices

### Record Keeping Setup
- [ ] Create accounting spreadsheet or use Wave (free accounting software)
- [ ] Set up invoice template
- [ ] Create receipt template
- [ ] Organize expense categories:
  - Equipment & maintenance
  - Transportation
  - Backing tracks / music
  - Marketing & promotion
  - Collaborator payments
